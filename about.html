Our Story

Crafting light and warmth since 2018

From Passion to Purpose

Lumière Candle Co. began in a small kitchen with a simple mission: to create beautiful, eco-friendly candles that bring warmth and comfort to every home. What started as a hobby quickly grew into a passion for perfecting the art of candle making.

Our founder, Sarah Mitchell, discovered her love for candle making while searching for natural alternatives to commercial candles that were filled with harmful chemicals. She spent years experimenting with different waxes, wicks, and fragrance combinations to develop the perfect formula.

Today, we're proud to hand-pour each candle in our workshop, using only the finest sustainable ingredients. Every candle tells a story of craftsmanship, sustainability, and the belief that everyone deserves a moment of peace and tranquility in their busy lives.

Our Sustainability Mission

At Lumière Candle Co., we believe that beautiful products shouldn't come at the expense of our planet. That's why sustainability is at the heart of everything we do.

  • 100% soy wax from renewable sources
  • Phthalate-free and toxin-free fragrances
  • Recyclable and reusable packaging
  • Carbon-neutral shipping options
  • 1% of sales donated to environmental causes
